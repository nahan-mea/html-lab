Courses Offered..

MEA Engineering College offers a diverse array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with essential skills for today's technological landscape. The undergraduate program includes a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), which spans four years and features specializations such as Computer Science, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, and Electronics and Communication Engineering. For postgraduate studies, the college provides a two-year Master of Technology (M.Tech) with specializations like VLSI Design, Software Engineering, Structural Engineering, Power Systems, and Data Science. Additionally, the Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program, lasting three years, focuses on areas such as Software Development, Networking, Database Management, and Web Technologies. The college prides itself on experienced faculty, industry collaborations that facilitate workshops and internships, and opportunities for research, all aimed at preparing students to meet the challenges of the engineering and technology sectors effectively.
